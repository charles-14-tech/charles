Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the battery last?

The ASUS ROG Zephyrus G14 offers up to 10-12 hours of battery life with general use (browsing, media, light productivity) and up to 4-6 hours under heavy workloads. For gaming, expect around 2-4 hours of battery life. A quick 15-minute charge provides up to 2 hours of additional use.

Are these laptops water-resistant?

No, the ASUS ROG Zephyrus G14 is not water-resistant. While it features a durable aluminum chassis, it does not have any official IP rating for water or dust resistance. It's important to keep it away from liquids to avoid damage to the internal components. Always use a protective case or bag when transporting it, especially in environments where water exposure is a risk.

How does it compare to other gaming laptops?

The ASUS ROG Zephyrus G14 stands out for its powerful AMD Ryzen 9 processor and NVIDIA RTX 4090 GPU in a compact 14-inch design, offering excellent portability at 3.5 lbs, impressive battery life of up to 12 hours with light use, and a high-quality 120Hz/144Hz WQXGA display, making it a strong contender compared to other gaming laptops like the Razer Blade 15 and Alienware X14.

What's included in the box?

The ASUS ROG Zephyrus G14 box contains the laptop, an AC power adapter, power cord, quick start guide, warranty information, ROG stickers, and may also include a USB-C to USB-A adapter or a protective sleeve, depending on the specific model and region.
